# Stromy Rebrand Assessment

Date: 2026-04-06
Mode: D - Full Rebrand
Status: In progress

## Why Rebrand

The current Stromy brand is a first draft, not an established market asset. There is no meaningful legacy equity to preserve beyond the name and a few conceptual signals. The goal is not a light modernization. The goal is to build a sharper, more distinctive, more credible identity for a B2B AI workflow and tooling firm serving corporates, agencies, and consulting businesses.

## Business Context

- Name: STROMY
- Name origin: syllable swap of "maestro"
- Core offer: workflow design, analytical systems, AI-enabled tooling, research/report production, operational copilots for knowledge work
- Audience: corporates, agencies, consulting firms, knowledge-heavy service businesses
- Brand ambition: borderless authority in AI-driven consulting and workflow infrastructure
- Most important touchpoints: website and HTML/report outputs, then presentations, documents, and tool UI

## Desired Brand Shift

Move away from "good first draft with editorial signals" toward a brand that feels:

- high-trust
- intellectually serious
- warm-authoritative
- crafted, not generic
- raw in materiality but refined in execution
- international, not local
- more distinctive in logo and typography

## Risk Analysis

- Brand equity at stake: low
- Stakeholder confusion risk: low
- Execution complexity: high, because the brand touches websites, reports, templates, and downstream assets
- Recommendation: proceed with full rebrand; the upside materially exceeds the risk

## Go / No-Go

Current recommendation: Go

Rationale:

- existing brand equity is weak
- business positioning is now clearer than when the first draft was produced
- the new identity needs to support premium trust in reports, proposals, and digital tools

## Brand Archaeology

### Current assets inventoried

- charter.json
- guidelines.md
- tokens.css
- logo set in `logos/`
- imagery set in `images/`
- PPTX, DOCX, HTML templates in `templates/`

### What is working

- "Stromy" is distinctive and has a strong origin story
- "Intelligence, Orchestrated." is directionally strong
- the orchestration / score / conducting concept is relevant
- the current system already leans editorial rather than startup-generic
- raw architectural / industrial imagery is strategically aligned

### What feels weak or generic

- the serif + sans pairing is competent but not ownable enough
- dark green + orange works, but currently reads more like a plausible palette than a decisive one
- the score-line logo system is clever but still feels like a first-pass device rather than a premium signature
- brutalist imagery direction is right in principle but not yet curated enough to feel uniquely Stromy
- the overall identity does not yet express enough personality or "specialness"

## Keep / Evolve / Retire

### Keep

- name: STROMY
- the maestro / orchestration story
- warm-serious tone
- international ambition
- editorial credibility target
- raw architectural / industrial material world as a starting point

### Evolve

- tagline
- archetype definition
- color system
- typography system
- motif system
- imagery treatment

### Retire

- assumption that the current logo structure must survive
- assumption that green/orange is fixed
- assumption that brutalist concrete alone is sufficient as a visual world

## Working Archetype Direction

Primary hypothesis:

- Sage x Creator, with a Magician undertone

Interpretation:

- Sage gives intellectual authority, clarity, rigor, and trust
- Creator gives crafted systems, originality, and design intent
- Magician remains present as the hidden engine: making complexity feel seamless

This is likely stronger than a pure Magician positioning, which risks reading as too abstract or too tech-mystical for the level of trust Stromy needs.

## Sacred Cows

Hard constraints currently confirmed:

- keep the name STROMY

Soft constraints currently likely:

- preserve the orchestration / maestro origin as a conceptual source
- keep the tagline only if something better does not emerge
- prioritize a wordmark-led identity with a secondary small mark or symbol

## Departing From

The old brand positioned Stromy as a dark editorial AI firm with a musical motif.

The new brand should position Stromy as a serious, high-end, globally credible intelligence and workflow partner whose visual system feels as considered as its analytical outputs.

## Open Decisions For Phase 0 / 1

- confirm the final archetype blend
- decide how far to move from green/orange
- decide whether the tagline stays or gets replaced
- define the exact personality spectrum positions
- define whether the visual world leans more "architectural editorial" or more "industrial craft"
