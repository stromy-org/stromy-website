# Stromy Rebrand - Phase 0 / 1 Working Strategy

Date: 2026-04-06
Status: Draft for review

## User-Locked Decisions

- Palette direction: `Deep Forest + Oxide`
- Display / body / mono: `Fraunces + Plus Jakarta Sans + IBM Plex Mono`
- Tagline: `Intelligence, Orchestrated.`
- Paper white: warm off-white, not bright white

These decisions are now treated as fixed for the next exploration round. Subsequent exploration should focus on motif systems, logo architecture, image treatment, and the exact balance between editorial and infrastructural expression.

## Working Assumptions

These assumptions were selected to keep momentum and can be revised after visual review.

- Archetype balance: Sage-led, Creator-supported, with a Magician undertone
- Primary tone: warm authority
- Visual world: balanced hybrid of architectural editorial and industrial craft
- Logo priority: wordmark-led identity, supported by a restrained secondary mark
- Wordmark casing: `STROMY` as the primary expression
- Tagline treatment: `Intelligence, Orchestrated.` remains the benchmark, not a sacred cow

## Departing From

The current brand already signals editorial seriousness, but it lacks enough ownable tension and distinction.

The new brand should:

- feel more premium and assured
- look more deliberate in typography
- carry a stronger sense of crafted systems
- move from "good concept" to "recognizable identity"

## Archetype

### Primary: Sage

Why:

- Stromy sells judgment, rigor, defensibility, and intelligence infrastructure
- clients need to trust the outputs, not just admire the technology
- the end products are reports, recommendations, and operational systems

### Secondary: Creator

Why:

- Stromy is not only an advisor; it builds bespoke systems and workflows
- the brand needs designed originality, not conservative consultancy sameness

### Undertone: Magician

Why:

- the promise is still transformation through orchestration
- clients should experience elegant outcomes while complexity stays behind the curtain

## Brand Essence

Stromy engineers serious intelligence systems so knowledge-driven businesses can decide and operate with sharper judgment.

## Positioning Statement

For corporates, agencies, and consulting businesses working in complex information environments, Stromy is the intelligence workflow partner that designs analytical systems, builds AI-enabled tooling, and structures high-trust outputs so teams can move from fragmented work to disciplined execution.

## Voice

Write like a highly prepared partner who has already done the analysis and is now giving the clearest version of the answer.

### Do

- lead with the conclusion
- use short, controlled sentences
- make technical points legible to non-specialists
- sound confident without sounding inflated
- describe systems and outcomes concretely

### Do not

- perform futurism
- use vague transformation language
- sound like startup marketing
- hide uncertainty behind jargon
- over-romanticize AI

## Visual Strategy Implications

### Typography

- use Fraunces for display to bring more signature and crafted authority
- use Plus Jakarta Sans for crisp report, UI, and website copy
- keep IBM Plex Mono for data, metadata, and technical credibility

### Color

- commit to Deep Forest + Oxide as the primary palette territory
- keep the palette dark, serious, and tactile
- use a warm paper tone instead of bright white
- let oxide function as a precise signal, not a decorative flood color

### Logo

- the wordmark should carry most of the identity weight
- any symbol or motif should feel structural, not illustrative
- the orchestration idea should appear through rhythm, tension, alignment, notation, grid, or signal behavior

### Imagery

- stay away from generic AI visual tropes
- bias toward architecture, materials, infrastructure, shadows, rhythm, and crafted surfaces
- balance rawness with editorial control
