Media relations in 2026 demands more than a contacts book and good instincts. Stromy brings data-driven intelligence to media strategy, providing clients with a comprehensive understanding of their media landscape across print, digital, broadcast, and social channels. Our platform monitors thousands of sources across multiple markets and languages, tracking not just mentions but the narratives, sentiment, and reach that determine whether coverage is a risk, an opportunity, or noise.

Our media analysis goes beyond what happened to explain why it matters. Weekly strategic briefings map the dominant narratives around a client's brand, sector, or issues, identify which journalists and outlets are most influential on specific topics, and flag emerging storylines before they reach critical mass. For clients operating across multiple markets, we provide cross-market narrative analysis that reveals how stories travel between regions and where messaging needs to adapt.

The result is a media relations function grounded in evidence rather than assumption. Clients pitch to the right journalists at the right time, respond to negative coverage with full context on how the narrative is evolving, and measure the impact of their media activity against clear benchmarks rather than vanity metrics.

## Key Outcomes

- Multi-market media monitoring across 1,200+ sources in multiple languages, covering print, digital, broadcast, and social channels
- Strategic narrative analysis that identifies how media stories form, travel, and evolve across markets and platforms
- Journalist and outlet influence mapping that helps clients direct engagement where it will have the greatest impact
- Rapid-turnaround briefings within 24 hours of breaking developments, providing context and recommended positioning
