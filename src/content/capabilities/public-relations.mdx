Public relations is undergoing a fundamental shift. Audiences are fragmented across platforms, attention spans are compressed, and the line between earned, owned, and paid media has blurred beyond recognition. Stromy equips PR teams with the intelligence they need to cut through this complexity — understanding not just where audiences are, but what they care about, how they consume information, and which messages resonate in which contexts.

Our platform provides real-time analytics on campaign performance, audience engagement, and message penetration across channels. Rather than waiting for end-of-campaign reports to learn what worked, PR teams can adjust strategy mid-flight based on live data. The system also identifies the advocacy voices, community influencers, and industry commentators whose endorsement carries genuine weight within specific audience segments — intelligence that is impossible to develop manually at scale.

We believe the best PR outcomes come from the intersection of creative strategy and rigorous intelligence. Our role is to provide the data and analytical infrastructure that makes strategic decisions sharper, faster, and evidence-based, while leaving the creative and relational dimensions to the communications professionals who know their clients best.

## Key Outcomes

- Real-time campaign analytics that track message penetration, audience engagement, and sentiment across channels as campaigns unfold
- Audience segmentation intelligence that reveals how different demographics consume information and respond to messaging approaches
- Influencer and advocacy mapping that identifies the voices with genuine impact within target audience segments
- Integrated measurement frameworks that connect PR activity to business outcomes, moving beyond impressions to meaningful impact metrics
