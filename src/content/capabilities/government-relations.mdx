Effective government relations depends on knowing what is happening across the legislative landscape before it becomes tomorrow's headline. Stromy provides real-time parliamentary monitoring and policy intelligence that covers federal, state, and local government across Australia, with expanding coverage in the Netherlands and broader European markets. Our AI systems ingest Hansard transcripts, committee reports, regulatory filings, and ministerial statements, then synthesise them into actionable briefings tailored to each client's policy interests.

We go beyond simple keyword alerts. Our platform understands context — connecting a question on notice in one jurisdiction to a related policy announcement in another, identifying the emerging themes that signal legislative intent before formal consultation begins. Government relations teams receive daily intelligence that is already analysed, prioritised, and mapped to their stakeholder landscape.

The result is a government relations function that operates with the coverage and speed of a much larger team. Clients identify policy risks earlier, engage with decision-makers at the right moment, and build submissions informed by cross-jurisdictional intelligence that their competitors simply do not have.

## Key Outcomes

- Cross-jurisdictional parliamentary monitoring covering 9 Australian legislatures and expanding international markets
- AI-synthesised daily briefings that surface the 5-10 developments that actually matter, from thousands of proceedings
- Early identification of emerging policy themes weeks before formal consultation or media coverage
- Stakeholder mapping that connects parliamentary activity to the specific decision-makers and committees relevant to each client
