Strategic decisions require evidence, not assumptions. Stromy's research and insights capability combines the scale of AI-powered data processing with the depth of expert analysis to deliver research that is both comprehensive and immediately actionable. We conduct media landscape analyses, stakeholder perception studies, policy environment assessments, and competitive intelligence projects that would take traditional research firms weeks — delivered in days.

Our research methodology leverages AI to process and structure large datasets — thousands of media articles, parliamentary proceedings, regulatory filings, or stakeholder communications — then applies experienced analytical judgement to interpret the patterns, identify the implications, and develop strategic recommendations. This hybrid approach means clients get the breadth of coverage that only machine processing can achieve, combined with the contextual understanding and strategic thinking that only human expertise provides.

Every research deliverable is designed around a specific decision the client needs to make. We do not produce generic landscape reports — we answer the question that matters, with evidence mapped to options and recommendations grounded in data. Whether the question is "Should we enter this market?", "How will this regulatory change affect our sector?", or "What do our stakeholders actually think about us?", the output is a clear, structured answer supported by evidence.

## Key Outcomes

- Rapid-turnaround research projects that deliver comprehensive analysis in days rather than weeks, powered by AI-assisted data processing
- Media landscape analyses covering thousands of sources across markets, with narrative mapping and sentiment trending
- Stakeholder perception studies that synthesise public statements, media commentary, and social signals into a coherent picture of how an organisation is perceived
- Policy environment assessments that map regulatory trajectories, identify risks and opportunities, and provide strategic recommendations for engagement
